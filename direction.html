Main entrance
Art Gallery Road directly across from the Art Gallery of NSW

The VIP entrance
Near Hospital Road, next to Guest Services.

We encourage you to use public transport. The closest train station is St James Station. It is approx. a 300m distance from the station to the main entrance.

There is parking available at The Domain Car Park (St Mary’s Road, Sydney). The discounted rate is only applicable to bookings made online or through the Wilson Parking app.

The drop off zone is located on Hospital Road and can be accessed via College St, St Marys Road and Prince Albert Road.

There will be road closures along Art Gallery Road, St Mary’s Ro ad, Prince Albert Road, Hospital Road, Macquarie St, St James Road, College St and Cathedral St at various times during the event. During these times, there will be increased amount of pedestrian and vehicle traffic in the local area, please expect short delays.
