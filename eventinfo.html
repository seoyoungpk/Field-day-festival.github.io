There a tent with low pressure mist cooling technology to stay and cool and comfortable.

party safe

If you feel unwell or see anyone looking unwell, come to party safe officer in bright pink shirts.

anything anytime

There is a cloakroom available. $5 per item there will be merch at anything anytime. You will be able to buy food and drinks for the duration of the event.

charged up

There is portable phone charging hire available from the ezycharge stall near anything anytime.

Caution

If you feel unwell, go to medical or to a party safe officer straight away. Who wearing bright pink shirts will help you. Also there are two chill out zones at the festival one at the front entry (outside the event) and one inside next to medical.

If something has happened to you, the chill out zone is a safe place with people trained and ready to listen to you, support and help you. If you witness something at the event, report it immediately to security or police on site, or at the medical or chill out zones.

No one likes to dance on rubbish, help make this event clean and green. Put your rubbish in the bin and respect the event boundaries. Recycle your can at the recycling point, get $1 back, and help keep the venue beautiful.
